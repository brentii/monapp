User Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

If you wish to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Log into your Facebook account.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  3. Click Apps and Websites.
  4. Find our app in the list of active apps and select it.
  5. Click Remove to delete the app and any associated data.
